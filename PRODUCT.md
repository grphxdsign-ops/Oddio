# OddioAI Product Context

## Register

product

## Product Purpose

OddioAI teaches beginner-intermediate musicians through source-referenced song practice, real-time feedback, and a witty coach that turns mistakes into useful drills.

## Users

- Self-taught guitar and piano learners who want songs, not dry lesson trees.
- Mobile-first learners practicing in bedrooms, dorm rooms, studios, and living rooms.
- Players who need clear correction without the app becoming stiff or judgmental in a boring way.

## Strategy

- Reference existing source sites for song notation. Do not copy, scrape, re-host, or generate copyrighted sheet music or tablature.
- Keep raw audio local by default. Send summaries, scores, and mistake metadata to tutor systems.
- Make practice feel conversational and funny, but every joke must include one correction and one next action.
- Start with guitar and piano. Keep the architecture ready for voice, more instruments, more keys, and real MIDI/audio adapters.

## Anti-References

- Generic AI-purple dashboards.
- Static lesson apps that feel like homework.
- Fake generated notation for songs the app has no rights to reproduce.
- Roast bots that insult without teaching.

## Quality Bar

- Source-reference policy is enforced in UI, tests, and reviews.
- Mobile controls must be reachable, legible, and stable on phone screens.
- E2E tests cover the learner path before merging major UI changes.
- Greptile, Impeccable, and Taste checks should be used as PR review lenses.
